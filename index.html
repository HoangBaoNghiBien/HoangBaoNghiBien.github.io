In this experiment, you will solve mini Sudoku puzzles.

The rules of Sudoku are to fill in all the digits so that no duplicates appear in any row, column or bold-bordered square.

This version of the interface uses the mouse or keyboard.

For the mouse:

  • To write a number, click on a handwritten number on the side, then click on a square.
  • Click Next to go to the next puzle.

For the keyboard:

  • Using mouse hover or arrow keyboard to choose cell.
  • Press a number key (1-4) to write a number.
  • Press 'e' to clear a cell.
  • Press 'c' to clear all.
  • Press 'Enter' to check the answer.
  • Press '<' to go to previous board.
  • Press '>' to go to next board.

Practice on puzzle #1. Then starting with puzzle #2, solve as many puzzles as quickly and correctly as you can.
